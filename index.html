Jonathan Skaza

PhD Student

University of California, Santa Barbara

I am a PhD student in the Dynamical Neuroscience program at UC Santa Barbara, where I study Mind and Machine Intelligence in the Vision and Image Understanding Lab.

Previously, I was a Computational Science Developer in the Cowley Group at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. Before that, I worked in industry at Panalgo, first as a Solutions Analyst and later as Associate Director of Product.

I hold an MSc in Biostatistics from the University of Michigan, where I conducted research in the Department of Psychiatry, and a BSc from Bryant University with a double major in Applied Mathematics & Statistics and Applied Economics.
