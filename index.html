I am a PhD student studying Mind and Machine Intelligence in the Vision and Image Understanding Lab and Dynamical Neuroscience program at UC Santa Barbara.

Previously, I worked as a Computational Science Developer in the Cowley Group at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. Prior to that, I served as a Solutions Analyst and subsequently Associate Director of Product in industry at Panalgo.

I received my MS in Biostatistics from the University of Michigan, during which I worked with the Department of Psychiatry, and BS with a double major in Applied Economics and Applied Mathematics & Statistics from Bryant University.
